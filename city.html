About Dehradun

Nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas, Dehradun is a picturesque city that serves as the capital of Uttarakhand. Known for its pleasant climate, prestigious educational institutions, and proximity to popular hill stations like Mussoorie and Rishikesh.

The city offers a perfect blend of urban amenities and natural beauty, making it an ideal destination for travelers seeking both adventure and tranquility. From ancient temples to modern attractions, Dehradun has something for every type of traveler.
